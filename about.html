-Who We Are

At Dadek Limited we specializes in acquiring goods, services, or work for other companies or organizations. Our primary objective is to help clients obtain the best value for their resources through efficient sourcing, negotiation, and purchasing processes. We typically work on behalf of businesses across various industries, such as manufacturing, construction, healthcare, and technology, managing the acquisition of raw materials, equipment, or services required for operations. Here are the key roles and functions of Dadek Limited:

1. Sourcing:

We identify potential suppliers and vendors who can provide the goods or services required. This involves researching and evaluating suppliers, ensuring they meet the required quality, cost, and delivery standards.

2. Negotiation:

We negotiate the terms of contracts, pricing, delivery schedules, and payment terms with suppliers. We aim to secure favorable terms for our clients, ensuring cost efficiency and reliability in the supply chain.

3. Purchasing and Ordering:

Dadek Limited handle the actual ordering process, including placing purchase orders, managing supplier relationships, and ensuring timely delivery. We also track inventory levels and reorder products as necessary.

4. Risk Management:

Dadek Limited assess potential risks in the supply chain, such as market volatility, delays, or geopolitical issues, and develop strategies to mitigate these risks. We Sometimes seek alternative suppliers or establish contingency plans.

5. Compliance and Sustainability:

Dadek Limited ensure that our clients adhere to legal, ethical, and environmental regulations when sourcing goods or services. We promote sustainable sourcing practices, such as choosing eco-friendly products or working with suppliers who adhere to ethical labor standards.

6. Cost Control and Budgeting:

We help clients control costs by optimizing the procurement process and seeking cost-effective alternatives. Effective procurement can have a significant impact on the overall profitability of the business.

7. Supplier Relationship Management:

Maintaining strong relationships with suppliers is crucial to ensuring continued access to quality products and services. Dadek Limited manage these relationships, handle any issues that arise, and ensure that performance standards are met.

WHAT WE DO

Strategic Sourcing: Identifying and partnering with the best suppliers globally to ensure cost-effective and reliable procurement.

Supply Chain Management: Managing the flow of goods and services from suppliers to end-users, ensuring efficiency and minimizing disruptions.

Vendor Management: Building and maintaining strong relationships with vendors to ensure quality, compliance, and timely delivery.

Procurement Consulting: Offering expert advice and strategies to optimize procurement processes and reduce costs.

Inventory Management: Implementing effective inventory control systems to maintain optimal stock levels and reduce holding costs.
